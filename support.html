Frequently Asked Questions

SafeURL is an online service that helps users verify the safety of URLs before visiting them. Our system checks URLs against a comprehensive database of known safe and malicious websites to protect you from phishing attempts, scams, and malware.

SafeURL uses a combination of database matching, domain analysis, and user reports to provide highly accurate results. However, no system is 100% perfect, especially with newly created malicious sites. We continuously update our database to improve accuracy.

Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. We do not store the full URLs you check unless they are reported as malicious. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect all user data. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Yes, SafeURL is fully responsive and works on all modern mobile devices. Premium and Business users can also access our mobile apps for iOS and Android for a more integrated experience.

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top navigation menu. Fill out the registration form with your email address and create a password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

You can change your email address in your account settings. Log in to your account, go to "Profile Settings," and update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

Our URL checker compares the URL you enter against our database of known safe and malicious websites. It also analyzes various aspects of the URL and website, such as domain age, SSL certificate, and reputation score, to determine its safety level.

An "Unknown" result means that the URL is not in our database of known safe or malicious websites. This often happens with new or rarely visited websites. We recommend exercising caution when visiting unknown URLs, especially if they ask for personal information.

Bulk URL checking is available for Premium and Business plan subscribers. With this feature, you can upload a CSV file containing multiple URLs or paste a list of URLs to check them all at once.

To upgrade your plan, log in to your account and go to "Subscription Settings." Select the plan you want to upgrade to and follow the payment instructions. Your new plan will be activated immediately after payment is processed.

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase by contacting our support team.

To cancel your subscription, log in to your account, go to "Subscription Settings," and click on "Cancel Subscription." Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle, after which it will not renew.

Our browser extension is available for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Log in to your Premium or Business account, go to "Browser Extension" in your dashboard, and follow the installation instructions for your browser.

API access is available for Premium and Business subscribers. In your account dashboard, go to "API Access" to generate your API key and view the documentation. The documentation includes examples and code snippets for integrating with our API.

If you're experiencing issues with our website, try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try using a different browser. If the problem persists, please contact our support team with details about the issue, including your device, browser, and any error messages you're seeing.
