Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and provide personalized content.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us track usage and improve performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your preferences and settings.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. By clicking "Accept Cookies", you agree to our Cookie Policy.
