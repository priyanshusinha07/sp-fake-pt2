Why Report Scams?

By reporting suspicious websites, you help:

  • Protect other internet users from falling victim
  • Improve our database of known threats
  • Contribute to a safer online environment
  • Alert authorities to new scam techniques

What Happens Next?

  1. Our security team reviews your report
  2. The URL is analyzed and verified
  3. If confirmed as malicious, it's added to our database
  4. If you provided your email, we'll notify you of the outcome

Already a Victim?

If you've already fallen victim to an online scam:

  • Change your passwords immediately
  • Contact your bank if financial information was compromised
  • Report identity theft to appropriate authorities
  • Consider credit monitoring services
